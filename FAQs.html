Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

At San Zivar we strive to provide the best possible customer service and to answer all of your questions, if you do not see your question do not hessite to contact our team at 647-465-7440 we will gladly answer all of your questions.
How are rates determined?

Rates are determined by many factors as every home is unique. We will send out a representative to evaluate and provide a flat rate for reoccurring cleaning (bi-weekly or monthly).

All other one time cleans are charged per hour- please refer to prices (Link to price tab) or Contact us at 647-465-7440.

What if Iâ€™m not satisfied with the service?

A representative will touch base with you after every service. If you are not satisfied with a certain area that was done we will gladly return the next day to retouch it.

What should I do to prepare for my home cleaning?

We ask that you please pick up all personal items in order to provide the team with a clutter free environment. If this is done we may not be able to fully complete the clean.

Who will be cleaning my home?

A team of professional house cleaners who have been trained by San Zivar will clean your home. Usually a team of two.

Do I need to provide house cleaning products and equipment?

No, we provide all cleaning products and bring our own equipment. However, if you have special cleaning products please advise the manager in order for us to properly instruct the team.

Will it cost the same each time you clean my house?

After the initial clean a flat rate will be determined based on the needs for the home and will not vary between visits.

Will you come on the same day and time each clean?

We know life gets busy! We will determine with you what day and time works on a reoccurring basis. This will provide consistency so you better prepare for our arrival. We try our best to be as flexible as possible and if a change needs to be made please reach out to the manager so an arrangement can be made.

What should I do with my pets when my house is being cleaned?

If there are pets in the house we ask that you prevent them from entering areas where cleaning is being done. For sanitary and safety reasons our cleaning professionals are not able to clean flea infested homes or pick up any animal excrement.

How do I pay for my home cleaning?

Debit and all major credit cards are accepted.

How do I schedule an appointment with you?

Feel free to give us a call at 647-465-7440, Send us an email or select a call back time found on our contact us page.
